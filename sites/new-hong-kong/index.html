Our Story

Family-Owned Since 2001

Tauranga became a great home for us to raise our family and sustain our business.

New Hong Kong Takeaways is a welcoming, family-owned business that has been part of the Greerton community since 2001. We've watched Greerton change and grow, but we stayed because of the people — our regulars, our neighbours, our friends.

Our goal has always been simple: delicious food that everyone can afford. We see every mistake as a chance to get better, and we strive to improve your experience with every visit.
