Our Purpose

Shopping with heart.

"Supporting Pregnancy Choice Tauranga."

Graced Opp Shop operates as part of Pregnancy Choice — a service providing free pregnancy tests, information, and support to women facing unplanned pregnancies.

Every purchase you make supports this work. You get beautiful pre-loved items at great prices, and a community service gets the funding it needs to keep running.

Come in, grab a coffee, find something you love, and know that your money is making a difference.
