The Studio

A place to breathe.

"Come to class expecting to decompress and press the reset button."

Yoga for Every Body is a boutique Balinese-feel yoga studio, located in a peaceful country setting between Bethlehem and Te Puna.

I teach people how to strengthen and lengthen, to observe their minds and to centre themselves. Classes leave you with a feeling of peace, and small numbers make for a truly personalised experience.

Whether you're brand new to yoga or have been practising for years, this is a space for you. Every body is welcome.
