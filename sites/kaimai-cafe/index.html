Our Story

The perfect stop.

"The gateway to Tauranga."

Situated on the lower Kaimai Ranges, Kaimai Cafe is the perfect place to enjoy a delicious range of homemade cuisine and great coffee in a peaceful, picturesque, rural atmosphere with plenty of free parking.

Everything is made on site, from our sweet and savoury cabinet items to the infamous date scones that keep people coming back. We also offer Heat & Eat frozen takeaway meals — perfect for busy families who still want a proper home-cooked dinner.

Proudly serving local tea and coffee from OCD Coffee and Webster's Tea.
